## Content Creators

### Monetizing Your Writing

**Scenario:** You write articles about Bitcoin, technology, or your area of expertise and want to earn directly from readers.

**How ZapTracker Helps:**

1. **Publish long-form content** using the NIP-23 article editor
2. **Track per-article performance** in the Content section
3. **Identify your most valuable topics** through Analytics
4. **Build a subscriber base** with Follow Lists

**Workflow:**

```
Write Article → Publish via ZapTracker → Share on Nostr
     ↓
Monitor Zaps in Real-Time → Analyze Performance
     ↓
Double Down on High-Performing Topics
```

**Key Metrics to Watch:**
- Sats per article
- Zap count vs. engagement quality (avg zap size)
- Repeat zappers (your true fans)

### Building a Paid Newsletter

**Scenario:** You want to create exclusive content for supporters while maintaining free public content.

**How ZapTracker Helps:**

1. **Post free teasers** as short notes
2. **Publish full articles** for zap-supported distribution
3. **Track top supporters** to identify potential premium subscribers
4. **Use campaigns** for special projects or series

**Strategy:**
- Free notes build awareness
- Quality long-form earns zaps
- Top 10% of zappers become your VIP list
- Offer early access or bonus content to consistent supporters

## Podcasters & Streamers

### Live Stream Monetization

**Scenario:** You run live streams and want real-time zap interaction with your audience.

**How ZapTracker Helps:**

1. **Display the Zap Feed** on a second monitor during streams
2. **See incoming zaps instantly** with sender info
3. **Track stream-by-stream earnings** in Analytics
4. **Thank zappers by name** as they come in

**Setup:**
1. Open ZapTracker's Zap Feed in a browser
2. Use OBS or stream software to capture a portion as overlay (optional)
3. Monitor incoming zaps during your stream
4. Acknowledge supporters in real-time

### Podcast Episode Funding

**Scenario:** You want to fund specific podcast episodes through community support.

**How ZapTracker Helps:**

1. **Create a Campaign** for each episode or series
2. **Set clear goals** - "Help us reach 100k sats to produce Episode 50"
3. **Share campaign links** in your podcast notes and social posts
4. **Track progress** and celebrate milestones publicly

**Campaign Structure:**
```
Episode Idea → Create Campaign → Promote on Show
     ↓
Community Zaps → Track Progress → Hit Goal
     ↓
Produce Episode → Thank Contributors
```

## Community Builders

### Curating Follow Lists

**Scenario:** You want to help newcomers find quality accounts in a specific niche.

**How ZapTracker Helps:**

1. **Create thematic Follow Lists** - "Best Bitcoin Artists," "Nostr Developers"
2. **Publish lists** for others to import
3. **Track adoption** as people follow your recommendations
4. **Build authority** as a community curator

**Follow List Ideas:**
- Geographic communities ("Nostr Japan")
- Professional niches ("Bitcoin Lawyers")
- Interest groups ("Photography on Nostr")
- Starter packs ("New to Bitcoin? Follow these 20")

See [GUIDE.md - Growing Your Audience](GUIDE.md#growing-your-audience) for step-by-step list creation.

### Running Community Challenges

**Scenario:** You want to incentivize community participation with zap rewards.

**How ZapTracker Helps:**

1. **Set up a Campaign** as a prize pool
2. **Define challenge rules** in the description
3. **Track contributions** from sponsors
4. **Distribute rewards** via wallet when challenge ends

**Example Challenge:**
```
"21 Days of Nostr" Challenge
- Campaign Goal: 210,000 sats prize pool
- Rules: Post daily for 21 days with #21DaysOfNostr
- Prizes: Top 3 participants split the pot
```

## Event Organizers

### Funding an Event

**Scenario:** You're organizing a Bitcoin meetup, conference, or workshop and need community funding.

**How ZapTracker Helps:**

1. **Create a Campaign** with your total budget goal
2. **Break down costs** in the description (venue, speakers, food)
3. **Set milestone amounts** - "At 500k sats we add live music"
4. **Thank sponsors** publicly as zaps come in

**Campaign Template:**
```
Title: [City] Bitcoin Meetup - [Month] 2024

Goal: 1,000,000 sats

Description:
Help us host the biggest Bitcoin meetup in [City]!

Budget Breakdown:
- Venue rental: 400,000 sats
- Speaker travel: 300,000 sats
- Food & drinks: 200,000 sats
- Swag & materials: 100,000 sats

Milestones:
- 250k: Event confirmed
- 500k: Catering added
- 750k: Speaker gifts funded
- 1M: After-party unlocked!
```

## Cross-Cutting Patterns

### The Supporter Flywheel

Regardless of use case, successful ZapTracker users follow this pattern:

```
Create Value → Receive Zaps → Analyze Performance
     ↓
Identify What Works → Create More Value → Grow Audience
     ↓
Engaged Audience → More Zaps → Repeat
```

### Key Success Metrics

| User Type | Primary Metric | Secondary Metric |
|-----------|---------------|------------------|
| Content Creator | Sats/Article | Unique Zappers |
| Podcaster | Sats/Episode | Campaign Completion % |
| Community Builder | Follow List Adoption | Community Size |
| Merchant | Transaction Volume | Repeat Customers |
| Event Organizer | Campaign Goal % | Attendee Zaps |
| Educator | Engagement per Lesson | Student Retention |
| Developer | Monthly Support | Contributor Count |
